Bonjour Mes Amis

By attending the University of North Carolina at Greensboro, Chloe was able to combine her design skills with technology and flourished as a graphic design major. Through learning the art of graphic design she found her true passion by taking her sketches and turning them into wallpapers.

She has done everything from design wallpaper to creating websites. Currently, she is woking for an IT company in Raleigh, North Carolina. There she is able to get the latest experience with technology and the opportunity to build captivating and unique websites for companies all over the country.

Today you can find Chloe in Raleigh, North Carolina with her Fiancé Josh and their first born “child” Theodore who is a 1 year old Great Pyrenees who is always a source of inspiration.
